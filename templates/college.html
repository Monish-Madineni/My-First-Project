Before selecting this college

  • Compare this college with at least three similar options.
  • Check previous round cutoffs for each branch.
  • Confirm tuition fee, transport, hostel, and scholarship details.
  • Review placement records for the branches you prefer.
  • Verify all information with official counselling sources.
Preference tip

Place branches in the order you actually want to study them. A higher-ranked branch is not always better if it does not match your interests or career plan.
