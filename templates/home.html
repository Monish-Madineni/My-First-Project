About the project

Built for TS EAPCET college selection

This project helps students explore Telangana engineering colleges using rank, branch, category, and gender-based cutoff data. It is meant to support shortlisting and comparison before filling web options.

Counselling checklist

Before final web options

  • Verify college and branch details on the official TS EAPCET counselling website.
  • Compare cutoff ranks across your category and gender.
  • Check tuition fee, location, hostel, transport, and affiliation.
  • Keep a balanced list of safe, target, and ambitious colleges.
